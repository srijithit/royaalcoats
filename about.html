About Royaal Coats

Established in 2012, Royaal Coats has been a leader in the powder coating industry, providing high-quality finishes for a wide range of metal products. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in the market.



We specialize in giving a new lease of life to metal items, ensuring they are not only protected from the elements but also look aesthetically pleasing. From industrial racks to delicate home decor, we handle every project with precision and care.
